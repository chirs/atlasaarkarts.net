Weather

Delhi enjoys beautiful weather in December. Sunny and mildly cool during the days, and can get cold at night. A jumper or woolen jacket is advisable.

Accommodation

Participants will be housed at one of the following locations

India Habitat Centre

India International Centre

India Islamic Centre

ITDC

YWCA

Currency

Rupees (INR)

Sightseeing

One day sightseeing will be arranged by the festival.

Otherwise, it is very easy to take taxi or an auto-rickshaw (three wheeler/tuk tuk), or hire a car (which comes with a driver) to get around the city.

Mobile Phones

Local 'pay as you go' or 'top up' SIM Cards are widely available, and can be purchased for less than 500 rupees. You can make international calls and access the internet with them.

Internet

Internet and broadband access available at most places of stay.
